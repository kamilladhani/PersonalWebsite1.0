It may not come as news that many studies have related violent video games to negative social behaviours. High school students who consistently play violent video games are predicted to show more aggression, hostility, and delinquency as they get older. It turns out it doesn’t matter what gender your child is, their grades in school, or what type of friends they have; The effects of violent video games on aggression are still evident regardless of these factors. And it isn’t just an increase in aggression that is worrisome; Studies show that these children are also less helpful than those who don’t play violent video games at all.
