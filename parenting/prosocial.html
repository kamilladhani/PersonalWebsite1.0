Interestingly, numerous studies have shown that not all video games will increase aggression in your child. Specifically, playing prosocial video games has been found to not only decrease aggression, but also increase helping. While the player aids other characters throughout the game, they are less likely to think in an aggressive manner and show an increase in positive moods. And it’s worth noting that these positive effects aren’t just short term; Consistently playing prosocial video games can help your child show more helpful behaviour over their lifetime.
